Core Learning Outcomes by Track

Challengers (W1 & W2) - The Hustle

🎯
Booth Engagement (W1)

Convert walk-by traffic into calendar bookings by quickly qualifying buyers and deploying 10-second hooks.

✉️
Post-Show Discipline (W2)

Execute the 24-hour follow-up rule and structure a 14/30/60/90-day multi-touch cadence to secure meetings post-CES.

🥂
Floor/Party Tactics (W2)

Proactively identify high-value targets at booths and in social/party settings, managing conversation and gracefully securing next steps.

Deal Makers (W3 & W4) - The Close

🔄
Adaptive Pitching (W3)

Diagnose buyer persona (CEO, CFO, CTO, etc.) in real-time and pivot the pitch content/tone to match stakeholder priorities.

🤝
Negotiation (W4)

Master the 'Give-Get' framework and US business protocols to handle objections and lock clear, concrete next steps/pilot commitments.

✍️
Deal Structuring (W4)

Practice structuring formal agreements (PoC, Enterprise Pilot, Partnership) to move discussions from interest to contract review.
